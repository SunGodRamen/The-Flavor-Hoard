## Ingredients
- 1 kg beef chuck, cubes
- 2 onions, 2 carrots, 2 celery, chunks
- 2 tbsp flour
- 500 ml Guinness stout
- 400 ml beef stock
- 2 bay leaves, thyme
- Salt, pepper, oil

## Instructions
1. Brown beef; add veg; stir flour 1 min.
2. Pour Guinness & stock; add herbs; simmer covered 2 h until tender. Season.
